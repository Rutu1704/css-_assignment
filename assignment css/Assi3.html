Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Slices Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Sliced bread
  • Sliced bread

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon until crispy.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place cheese, bacon, and tomato slices between two slices of bread.

4. Grill sandwiches on a skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!
